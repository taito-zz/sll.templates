Log in as Manager::

    >>> browser.login(TEST_USER_NAME, TEST_USER_PASSWORD)

Go to Folder::

    >>> browser.open('/folder')
    >>> 'Make micro site' in browser.contents
    True
    >>> 'Unmake micro site' in browser.contents
    False

    >>> print browser.bycss('#portaltab-index_html')
    --- 1 ---
    <li id="portaltab-index_html" class="plain"><a href="http://nohost/plone" title="">Home</a></li>

Make micro site::

    >>> browser.getLink('Make micro site').click()
    >>> 'Make micro site' in browser.contents
    False
    >>> 'Unmake micro site' in browser.contents
    True

    >>> print browser.bycss('#portaltab-index_html')
    --- 1 ---
    <li id="portaltab-index_html" class="selected"><a href="http://nohost/plone/folder" title="">Home</a></li>

Unmake micro site::

    >>> browser.getLink('Unmake micro site').click()
    >>> 'Make micro site' in browser.contents
    True
    >>> 'Unmake micro site' in browser.contents
    False

    >>> print browser.bycss('#portaltab-index_html')
    --- 1 ---
    <li id="portaltab-index_html" class="plain"><a href="http://nohost/plone" title="">Home</a></li>
